What is National Peace Consciousness Month?

National Peace Consciousness Month or also known as "Proclamation no. 675, s. in 2004". National Peace Consciousness Month has been going on for 20 years since it was declared! Just 2 weeks ago, RTVMalacanang streamed the Opening Ceremony of the 2024 National Peace Consciousness in YouTube for this year. National Peace Consciousness Month was declared to instill greater consciousness and understanding among the Filipino people on the comprehensive peace process to strengthen and sustain popular support for and participation in this effort.

Credits: National Libary of the Philippines / Visit the Website



Why is National Peace Consciousness Month important?

National Peace Consciousness Month or NPCM is important because it helps Filipinos become more aware of peace and its significance. It serves to inform and educate people about the importance of achieving and maintaining peace. By raising awareness and providing education on peace initiatives, National Peace Consciousness Month plays a crucial role in fostering a deeper understanding and commitment to peaceful coexistence.
